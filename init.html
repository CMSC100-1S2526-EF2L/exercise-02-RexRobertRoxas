Autobiography
Born in Cebu City, Cebu, I first found programming through Scratch, a program I found on my sister’s laptop. I took some time experimenting on it, and found my love for programming then. However, my love for developing emerged much later. In Grade 7, we were taught HTML and CSS, and in Grade 9, we were taught JavaScript and PHP. Yet, just before I started Grade 10, I developed a “To-Do list” web app, made with HTML and PHP, with MySQL as my database. It was then that I discovered that I loved to develop projects.
